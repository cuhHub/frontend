About Us

cuhHub is a gameserver-hosting community for the games Stormworks and Archean.
We started back in 2022 with a unique minigames server only on Stormworks. Since then, we have adapted to the playerbase
and have released enjoyable, quality-assured gamemodes for all to enjoy.

We are always open to suggestions, join our Discord server to get in touch!

Servers we offer include:

Sandbox

Sandbox, a server featured on both Stormworks and Archean where players can spawn vehicles and play together.

Comes with unique features such as safe zones, danger zones, anti-lag, and so on!

Outbreak

A server with a never-seen before zombies gamemode specifically for Stormworks. It features zombie apocalypse gameplay where you must use your vehicles to fight against zombies.

Complete with an economy system, missions and more!

Missions

A server dedicated to career-like gameplay. Earn money by transporting passengers, transporting cargo, towing vehicles and more!

Build your reputation with companies or go freelancer-style, but at the end of the day, become a known name!

This server is exclusive to Stormworks.
