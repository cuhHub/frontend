What is Sandbox?

Sandbox is our most popular server where you are bounded by no limits.
Spawn vehicles, fight with other players, survive disasters, and much more!

Danger Zones

Unlike any other Stormworks server, we have zones where dangers, like disasters, are present and frequently spawn. You can use these areas to test your vehicles against things like tsunamis!

Safe Zones

Sandbox, unlike other servers where a ?pvp command exists, uses a safe zone system.
Vehicles and players within safe zones are fully protected from damage, allowing you to be safe from other players.

Companions

Need a crew to populate your vehicle? This is where our companion system comes into play!
Sandbox allows you to spawn companions (NPCs) via the ?companion command.

Landmarks

Our Sandbox server comes with landmarks - special areas marked around the map.
Teleport to them with ?tp or navigate to them manually - go explore!

User-Friendly

Our servers are designed to be as fun and convenient as possible, and Sandbox is no exception.
With plenty of commands to manage your experience, you can easily do what you want.

Anti-Lag

Sandbox comes with a custom anti-lag system to ensure laggy vehicles do not stick around.
This allows for the server to remain at high performance, keeping your experience smooth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are the rules?

You can find the rules for Sandbox, as well as cuhHub in general, in our Discord server.

How do I join Sandbox?

Joining is simple, simply follow these steps:

  1. Open Stormworks.
  2. Go to the multiplayer server browser.
  3. Search for "cuhHub Sandbox".
  4. Join the server from there.

How do I see all available commands?

Type ?help in chat to see all commands and their descriptions.

Is PvP allowed in safe zones?

PvP is not allowed in safe zones and is intentionally prevented. However, please report people who attempt to PvP within safe zones through our support channel in our Discord server.

Can I use the Sandbox code in my own server?

Unfortunately, Sandbox's code is not open-source and can only be used on cuhHub's servers.

My vehicle got despawned, why?

Sandbox has a custom anti-lag system to ensure laggy vehicles do not stick around.
If your vehicle was despawned, it is usually due to the anti-lag system being triggered or you have too many vehicles.
If you have too many vehicles, use the ?despawn command.
If the anti-lag system is the reason your vehicle was despawned, it will tell you exactly what happened in chat and via notifications.

How do I repair my vehicle?

You can repair a vehicle using the ?repair (vehicle ID) command.
This will respawn the vehicle in place and remove all damage.
Note that this does not reattach sub-bodies such as missiles.

If you only want to refuel and recharge your vehicle, use ?replenish instead.

My vehicle flipped over, do I have to respawn it?

No. You can use the ?flip (vehicle ID) command to flip your vehicle upright.
This allows you to continue without respawning.

How do I get the IDs of my vehicles?

You can find your vehicle IDs in two ways:

  • Look at the UI in the top-left of your screen - your spawned vehicles and their IDs are shown there.
  • Use the ?vehicles command to list all your spawned vehicles and their IDs.

What are companions?

Companions are simply spawnable NPCs. They allow you to, for example, simulate having a crew without relying on other players.

Spawn companions using ?com or ?com (amount).
To remove all companions, use ?rcom.
