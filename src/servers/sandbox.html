What Is Sandbox?

Sandbox is our most popular server where you are bounded by no limits.
Spawn vehicles, fight with other players, survive disasters, and much more!

Danger Zones

Unlike any other Stormworks server, we have zones where dangers, like disasters, are present and frequently spawn. You can use these areas to test your vehicles against things like tsunamis!

Safe Zones

Sandbox, unlike other servers where a PvP toggle command exists, uses a safe zone system. Vehicles and players within safe zones are fully protected from damage, allowing you to be safe from other players.

Companions

Need a crew to populate your vehicle? This is where our companion system comes into play! Sandbox allows you to spawn companions (NPCs) via the ?companion command.

Landmarks

Our Sandbox server comes with landmarks - special areas marked around the map. Teleport to them with ?tp or navigate to them manually - go explore!

User-Friendly

Our servers are designed to be as fun and convenient as possible, and Sandbox is no exception. With plenty of commands to manage your experience, and features like vehicle button actions, you can easily do what you want.

Anti-Lag

Sandbox comes with a custom anti-lag system to ensure laggy vehicles do not stick around. This allows for the server to remain at high performance, keeping your experience smooth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about our Sandbox server.
You may find an answer to your question here.

Where are the rules?

You can find the rules in our Discord server.
If you see a player breaking the rules, please report them in our Discord.

How do I join?

Joining is simple. Simply launch Stormworks, open the in-game server browser, and search for "cuhHub".
From there, click the join button next to our Sandbox server.

Where can I find the in-game commands?

Use ?help in-game to see a list of available commands.
Additionally, see our Getting Started page for more information.

Is PvP allowed?

Yes! PvP is allowed outside of safe zones.
If someone attacks you in a safe zone, please report them in our Discord server.

Why did my vehicle get despawned?

Our addon may despawn your vehicle if it is deemed too laggy on spawn, or you reached a limit.
You will be notified of the exact reason your vehicle despawned in chat and on the right-side of your screen via notifications.
If you reached the vehicle limit, use ?c to despawn your vehicles.

My vehicle flipped, do I have to respawn it?

For situations like this, we provide useful commands to get your vehicle back on track.
Use ?flip (vehicle ID) to flip your vehicle upright.

How do I suggest a feature?

We love hearing suggestions from our community!
Join our Discord server and head to the suggestions channel to share your ideas.

How do I get the IDs of my vehicles?

Use ?vehicles in-game to see a list of your vehicles and their IDs.
You can also see the ID of a vehicle by getting close and looking at it.
Alternatively, you can refer to the vehicle list UI on your screen.

How do I repair, recharge, refuel and rearm my vehicle?

Use ?repair (vehicle ID) and ?replenish (vehicle ID) to repair or recharge/refuel/rearm your vehicle respectively.

What are vehicle button actions?

Vehicle button actions allow you to operate on your vehicle using buttons rather than commands.
For example, you can have a button named ch_repair, and pressing it will repair your vehicle just like using ?repair.
You can see a list of available button actions by getting close to your vehicle and looking at it.
