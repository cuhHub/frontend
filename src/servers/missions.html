What Is Missions?

Welcome to the ultimate Stormworks career experience.

Missions is our career server for Stormworks focused on progression, challenge and replayability. Take on ranked, reputation-gated missions with support for co-operative play. Join companies for exclusive, high-paying missions, and build your career through passenger and cargo operations across the map.

With a custom per-player economy, mission chains, companies, insightful UI and more, every mission pushes you to be the best Stormworker.

Missions

Take on missions across the map with support for co-op play. Complete missions to earn money, reputation and climb the ranks. With a variety of mission types and clear progression, you will never get bored.

Companies

Unlike any other career experience in Stormworks, our Missions server features companies which you can join to access field-specific, high-paying missions. Use ?companies in-game to see a list of companies and their requirements.

XP System

With our custom XP system, you can earn XP for completing missions and other activities on the server. Rank up to unlock even more missions and content.

Reputation System

Our reputation system forces you to focus on completing missions. Failing or cancelling missions will lower your reputation, restricting your access to missions and companies. Stay reliable!

Per-Player Money

Our custom per-player economy allows you to focus on your own progression and career without worrying about other players occupying your money. Build your own fortune at your own pace.

Diverse Cargo

Our cargo missions feature a wide variety of cargo types, from SIBTaT trailers, to small packages, even to ISO containers. Each cargo type has its own unique handling requirements, adding an extra layer of logistics and fun to our missions.

User-Friendly

Our servers aim to guide you and make your experience as smooth as possible, and Missions is no exception. With a custom UI, helpful occasional tips, and a focus on user-friendliness, you can focus on the fun parts of the server without getting confused.

Anti-Lag

Just like Sandbox, Missions comes with a custom anti-lag system to ensure laggy vehicles do not stick around. This allows for the server to remain at high performance, keeping your experience smooth.

...and so much more!

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about our Missions server.
You may find an answer to your question here.

Where are the rules?

You can find the rules in our Discord server.
If you see a player breaking the rules, please report them in our Discord.

How do I join?

Joining is simple. Simply launch Stormworks, open the in-game server browser, and search for "cuhHub".
From there, click the join button next to our Missions server.

Where can I find the in-game commands?

Use ?help in-game to see a list of available commands.
Additionally, see our Getting Started page for more information.

How do I start a mission?

Locate the mission you would like to start on the map and head to its starting location.
Upon arriving, type ?start to start the mission.
You can cancel or leave the mission at any time with ?cancel.
Please note that if a player has already started the mission, attempting to start it will send an invite to the player ("host") instead.

What are companies?

Companies are one of your primary goals in Missions. They are specialised groups you can join to access exclusive, high-paying missions.
Companies are often focused on different fields, so pick the one you want! Note that companies are very expensive to join, so you will need to complete missions and earn money to be able to join one.

Use ?companies in-game to see a list of companies, their requirements and their benefits.
Additionally, you can use ?join [company name] to join a company once you meet its requirements.

Note: [company name] can be partial, like a Google search. "jaKe" would be the same as "Jake Logistics".

Can I switch companies?

Yes, you can switch companies at any time. Use ?leave to leave your current company, then use ?join [company name] to join another company.
Note: [company name] can be partial, like a Google search. "jaKe" would be the same as "Jake Logistics".

Why can't I join a mission?

Missions have various requirements such as rank, reputation, company membership and money (for the entrance fee).
If you do not meet any of a mission's requirements, you will not be able to join it. The gamemode will notify you of this, often through notifications on the right of your screen.

How can I find missions?

You can find missions on the map or by using the ?missions command to see a list of available missions and their information.
Additionally, the mission icons on the map are color-coded!

  • Red icons are for missions that are not available to you (e.g. you do not meet requirements).
  • Grey icons are for missions that are not available in general, or in-progress (but you do meet requirements).
  • Any other colour icons represent the type of mission and all mean that you are eligible to partake.

I've lost all my money, what do I do?

Look for a bright green icon on the map. These represent loan missions where an NPC will generously give you just enough money to reach the money you started with when you first joined. These only work if you have less money than your starting money, so they are a safety net to ensure you can always keep playing and progressing.

Why did my vehicle get despawned?

Our addon may despawn your vehicle if it is deemed too laggy on spawn, or you reached a limit.
You will be notified of the exact reason your vehicle despawned in chat and on the right-side of your screen via notifications.
If you reached the vehicle limit, use ?c to despawn your vehicles.

How do I suggest a feature?

We love hearing suggestions from our community!
Join our Discord server and head to the suggestions channel to share your ideas.

How do I get the IDs of my vehicles?

Use ?vehicles in-game to see a list of your vehicles and their IDs.
You can also see the ID of a vehicle by getting close and looking at it.
Alternatively, you can refer to the vehicle list UI on your screen.
