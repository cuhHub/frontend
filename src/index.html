What To Expect

Here's what to expect when playing on cuhHub.

Unique Gamemodes

We offer addictive gamemodes that you won't find anywhere else on Stormworks. From custom missions to zombie survival, we have it all.

Friendly Community

Our servers are full of active, friendly players. You can find a friend in every corner.

Quality Moderation

Our servers are well-moderated thanks to autonomous and manual volunteer work.

Amazing Support

Alongside our servers, we have an active support team that is always ready to help you. You can find us in our Discord server.

High Performance

We take care to make sure our servers have the best performance possible. Our servers also come with anti-lag features to prevent laggy vehicles staying around.

Servers

At cuhHub, we offer a variety of unique servers with their own custom gamemodes for you to enjoy. Learn more about them below.

Sandbox

Our flagship server which comes with our own custom features such as companions, safe zones, danger zones, landmarks and more! Join and see what the hype is about!

Learn More

Outbreak

An economy-based server where you must survive against zombies and other players. Manage your hunger, loot airdrops, fight zombies and earn money!

Missions

A server that offers a custom, mission-based gamemode that knocks Stormworks' vanilla career out of the park. Work for companies or as a freelancer and complete missions to earn money and reputation!

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cuhHub.
You may find an answer to your question here.

Where is the Discord?

You can join our Discord using this link. You can get support from our staff and community there.

How do I apply for a staff or tester position?

Head over to the Applications page. Note that these positions are purely voluntary!

Is cuhHub's code open-source?

Most of our code is closed-source to prevent other servers copying us, but we do have some open-source code for people to check out in our GitHub.

Where are the servers located?

The servers are hosted in Finland to prvoide low latency to players across Europe. We may expand to other regions in the future!

I got moderated (banned, kicked, etc.), what do I do?

If you were moderated, you broke a server rule. Please read the rules in the Discord server.
If you think you were moderated unfairly, please contact us in the Discord server.

How do I report a player?

You can report a player in the Discord server.

How do I get support?

You can get support from our staff and community in the Discord server.
