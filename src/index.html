How do I join?

Step 1

Start up Stormworks via your Steam library (or whatever app you use).

Step 2

Click the "Multiplayer" button on the main menu.

Step 3

Enter the "Internet" tab, search "cuhHub" and choose a server, then click the join button.

FAQ

How do I apply for staff or a tester position?

Head over to the applications page to apply.

How long has cuhHub been around for?

cuhHub has been around since 2022. We started with a niche minigames server and evolved from there.

Is there a Discord server for cuhHub?

Yup! You can join our Discord through this link. You can get support from our staff and community there.

Is cuhHub's code open-source?

Most of our code is closed-source to prevent other servers copyign us, but we do have open-source code for people to check out here.

Where are the servers located?

Our servers are hosted in Finland to provide low latency to players across Europe.
We may expand to other regions in the future!

I got moderated (banned, etc), what do I do?

If you were moderated, it was likely for breaking the server rules. Please read over the rules available in the Discord server..
If you believe it was a mistake, you can also appeal it through the Discord server via the support channel.

How do I report a player?

You can report a player via our support channel in our Discord server..

Where can I suggest features or report bugs?

You can provide feedback via our feedback channel in our Discord server..

Where can I ask questions?

You can ask questions in our Discord server.. The community and staff will be happy to assist! If your question requires privacy, you can DM a staff member or make a ticket in our support channel.
